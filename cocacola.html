Brief

We developed an engaging, interactive Christmas-themed campaign for Coca-Cola, allowing fans to experience the holiday spirit in a fun, immersive way. This not only boosted brand visibility but also fostered deeper emotional connections with consumers, driving both brand loyalty and holiday sales.
