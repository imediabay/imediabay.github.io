FANTA Summer Campaign

How to play?

 

Click the "JOGAR AGORA >" button to enter the game.

 Swap adjacent fruits to create a line of three or more matching colors.
 

Matched fruits will disappear, and new fruits will fall into place.

 Earn points for each match and complete levels to unlock rewards.(If using a mouse, please use the mouse cursor instead of your finger)
Brief

This summer, we created an engaging, interactive campaign for Fanta, offering a fun and refreshing experience that resonated with their audience. By tapping into playful, immersive content, we boosted brand awareness, strengthened customer loyalty, and drove deeper connections with a younger, vibrant demographic eager for fresh and exciting experiences.
